# ZenPaws — Brand Identity & Style Guide

Welcome to the foundation of **ZenPaws**, a premium pet wellness brand focusing on ergonomic health, anxiety relief, and smart lifestyle integration for cats and dogs.

---

## 1. Brand Essence & Positioning
*   **Mission:** To design and curate products that bring harmony, health, and stress-free comfort to pets and their owners.
*   **Core Pillars:**
    *   **Ergonomic Care:** Protecting pets' joints and digestion.
    *   **Anxiety Relief:** Creating safe spaces and sensory enrichment.
    *   **Smart Simplicity:** Easy-to-clean, modern designs that fit beautifully in any home.
*   **Target Audience:** Busy urban professionals, "pet parents" (ages 22-45) who view their pets as children and are willing to invest in premium wellness products.

---

## 2. Visual Identity System

### A. Color Palette
To project calm, premium wellness, we use a warm organic-meets-modern palette:
*   **Primary (Deep Forest):** `#1F3E3D` (RGB: 31, 62, 61) — Represents nature, trust, and premium quality.
*   **Secondary (Soft Sage):** `#8EAC9D` (RGB: 142, 172, 157) — Calm, organic, and inviting.
*   **Accent (Warm Terracotta):** `#D28C70` (RGB: 210, 140, 112) — Warmth, playfulness, and highlights.
*   **Background (Alabaster White):** `#F7F5F0` (RGB: 247, 245, 240) — Pure, clean, minimalist canvas.
*   **Text (Charcoal):** `#2A2A2A` (RGB: 42, 42, 42) — High readability.

### B. Typography
*   **Headings:** *Outfit* or *Playfair Display* (Google Fonts) — Elegant, modern geometric sans-serif.
*   **Body Copy:** *Inter* or *DM Sans* (Google Fonts) — Neutral, highly readable, and clean.

### C. Voice & Tone
*   **Calm & Empathetic:** We understand pet struggles (anxiety, joint pain, picky eating) and offer solutions like a trusted friend.
*   **Expert but Accessible:** Scientific benefits (e.g., "orthopedic foam alignment", "digestive health angles") are explained in simple, human terms.
*   **Playful & Warm:** Subtle pet-related humor (without being overly childish or cheesy).
